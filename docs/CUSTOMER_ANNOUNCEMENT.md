# What's coming next — for our customers

Hi! We're building something new, and we want to tell you what it means
**for you** — in plain numbers.

---

## A new website to order your pizza

You'll soon be able to order from us on a real website, not just on
WhatsApp. WhatsApp keeps working — the website is just another option,
faster for some things.

### What you get

**1. Order in about 1 minute, instead of 3.**
Today, ordering on WhatsApp takes around **3 minutes** of back-and-forth
typing. On the new website, picking your pizza and checking out will take
about **1 minute** — roughly **3× faster**, and around **2 minutes saved
per order**.

**2. The full menu, on one screen.**
You'll see **100% of our pizzas, sizes, crusts and extras** at a glance,
with photos and prices. No more scrolling through chat to remember what
we offer.

**3. Real-time order tracking, 24 hours a day.**
A live status page shows exactly where your order is — *received → preparing
→ out for delivery → delivered* — updated in real time. No need to ask
"is my order coming?" — you'll just see it.

**4. Reorder your favorites in 1 click.**
Your last orders are saved. The next time you want the same pizza, it's
**one click instead of retyping everything**.

**5. Saved addresses.**
Save up to several delivery addresses (home, work, your parents' place).
Picking one at checkout takes **1 tap**.

**6. Order whenever it's convenient — even at 3 a.m.**
The site is online **24/7**. You can place an order at any hour, and we'll
prepare it as soon as the kitchen opens.

---

## Coming a little later

- **Loyalty points.** Roughly **1 point for every R$10 you spend**, redeemable
  for discounts and free items. Example: **100 points = R$ 20 off**.
- **Birthday gift.** A one-time discount coupon, automatically, the week of
  your birthday.
- **Promotions and coupons.** Codes you can use directly at checkout — no
  more asking the bot if a promotion is still valid.
- **Email receipt.** Optional — a clean PDF receipt sent to your email after
  every order.

---

## What stays the same

- WhatsApp ordering still works exactly as today.
- Your phone number is your account. If you order on the website with the
  same number you used on WhatsApp, you'll see **all your past orders**
  unified in one history.
- Our prices, our menu, our delivery zones — all unchanged.

---

## In short

- Place an order in **~1 minute** instead of ~3.
- See the **full menu on one screen**, not by scrolling chat.
- **Live order tracking, 24/7** — no more asking "is my order ready?".
- **Saved addresses** — pick one with a single tap.
- **Reorder in 1 click** instead of retyping everything.
- **Earn 1 loyalty point per R$10 spent** — rewards for ordering with us.

We're doing this so ordering from us is **faster, easier, and more
rewarding** for you.

Thank you for your trust — see you soon on the new site! 🍕
